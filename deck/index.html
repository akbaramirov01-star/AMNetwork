The Problem

$600B a year. Zero transparency.

Zakat is a pillar of Islam — 1.8 billion Muslims give every year. Yet there is no infrastructure to verify where that money actually goes.

  • ✕No global tracking. $600B flows yearly through unaccountable intermediaries.
  • ✕Donors can't verify. They never know if their Zakat reached anyone.
  • ✕Recipients are invisible. Millions in need have no dignified way to be seen.
  • ✕Trust is falling. Fraud and middlemen erode confidence in giving.
The Solution

Blockchain proof. AI verification.

AM Network records every Zakat transfer permanently on-chain and verifies every recipient with AI scoring and a local oracle network of imams and NGOs.

For Donors

  • Give with full certainty and on-chain proof
  • See exactly where every dollar lands
  • Calculate & pay annual Zakat in one place

For Recipients

  • Apply privately and with dignity
  • AI scores each case 0–100 across 11 categories
  • Local imam or NGO verifies eligibility
