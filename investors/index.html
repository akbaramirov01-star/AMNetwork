Problem & Solution

$600 billion. Zero transparency.

Every year, Muslims around the world pay Zakat — a religious obligation. But there is no reliable way to verify where that money goes.

The Problem

  • No global Zakat tracking infrastructure
  • Millions of eligible recipients are invisible to the system
  • Donors cannot verify where funds go
  • Fraud, misallocation, and middlemen absorb funds
  • No Sharia-certified on-chain solution exists

Our Solution

  • Every transaction recorded permanently on Base blockchain
  • AI scoring of recipients (0–100) across 11 categories
  • Local oracle network: imams + NGOs verify eligibility
  • Sharia-compliant ujrah model — no riba, no speculation
  • 8 languages, global from day one
