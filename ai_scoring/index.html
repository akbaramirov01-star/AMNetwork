How AM Zakat.ai Works
8 scoring components. Zero human bias.
01
Income & Need
Per-capita income vs. regional poverty threshold. 55+ country-specific baselines.
02
Assets & Nisab
Automatic nisab check at $5,100 (85g gold). Asset depletion signals extreme distress.
03
Family Structure
Dependents, minor children, elderly, widows, single parents, sole breadwinners.
04
Housing & Shelter
From homeless / refugee camp to stable owned home. 8 status levels.
05
Employment
Unemployment duration, seasonal / informal / part-time work weighed against income.
06
Health & Medical
Disability, chronic illness without access, terminal illness, sick dependents.
07
Asnaf Match
15 Islamic jurisprudence categories automatically identified per applicant.
08
Crisis & Emergency
Natural disaster, conflict zone, sudden job loss, domestic violence — automatic escalation.
