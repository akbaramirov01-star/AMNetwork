### Problem Statement:
```
Less than $25B of the $600B annual Zakat obligation is formally distributed, due to 
three core problems:
1. Lack of transparency — donors cannot verify recipient legitimacy
2. No verifiable distribution — funds disappear into charity "black boxes"  
3. No fraud prevention — fake recipients inflate aid claims

AM Network solves this by combining AI recipient scoring with blockchain-verified 
transactions and a local oracle network (mosque imams + NGO partners).
```

### Solution:
```
- AI scoring: Each recipient scored 0–100 across 15 eligibility criteria
- Smart contracts on Base blockchain: Every transaction immutably recorded
- Oracle network: Local imams/NGO reps verify physical presence
- Ujrah model: 1–2.5% service fee — Sharia-certified, transparent
- Multi-language: Platform available in 8 languages for global reach
```
