How it works

Simple for donors.
Life-changing for recipients.

1

You give — zakat or sadaka

Connect your wallet or pay with card. Choose a region, a cause, or let the algorithm distribute where need is highest. Every payment is locked in a smart contract — not in our bank account.

Zakat · Sadaka · Any amount
2

AI screens every recipient

Recipients upload documents. Our AI scores their need from 0–100 based on family size, income level, medical situation, and regional cost of living. No personal data stored on-chain — only an anonymous hash.

AI verified
3

Local oracles confirm

A trusted local verifier — an imam or social worker — reviews the AI score and confirms. For sadaka under a threshold, this step is automatic. For large zakat distributions, the DAO community votes.

Fast for sadaka
4

Funds arrive automatically

The smart contract transfers funds directly to the recipient's wallet. No intermediary touches the money. The entire flow is publicly visible on the blockchain — forever.

On-chain forever
5

You see proof

Click a link. See exactly which anonymous profiles received your money, in which countries, on which date. Download a zakat certificate for your records.
