How it works

Simple to give. Life-changing to receive.

Five transparent steps — from your donation to verified delivery.

01
💰

You give

Send zakat or sadaka in any amount. Your funds are locked immediately in a smart contract on Base blockchain — not in our bank account.

Zakat · Sadaka
02
🤖

AI scores need

Recipients submit encrypted documents. Our AI model scores each case 0–100 based on family size, income, medical conditions and regional cost of living. Personal data stays off-chain.

AI Verified
03
🕌

Oracle verifies

A trusted local verifier — a certified imam or licensed social worker — reviews the AI assessment and confirms eligibility.

Sharia-certified
04
⚡

Automatic transfer

The smart contract executes autonomously: funds flow directly from the pool to the recipient's wallet. Zero human intermediaries. Zero possibility of diversion.

Base · Solana
05
🔍

You receive proof

A permanent blockchain record is created showing exactly which verified profiles received your funds, in which countries, on which date — forever on-chain.
